Our Mission

Our mission is to empower stakeholders by providing accurate data and insights on price anomalies and trends. By leveraging the power of machine learning, we aim to contribute to the stability and transparency of the food industry in Malaysia.

What We Do

  • Anomaly Detection: Identifying unusual price trends using advanced algorithms like Gaussian Distribution, Isolation Forest, and Random Forest Regression.
  • Price Prediction: Predicting future price trends for food ingredients across various Malaysian states.
  • Price Comparison: Offering state-based price comparisons to assist in understanding market dynamics.

Our Approach

We employ the Waterfall methodology to develop our system, ensuring systematic processes from requirements gathering to deployment. Our machine learning models are optimized to provide accurate predictions, driven by key performance metrics such as RMSE and MSE.

Why It Matters

The stability of raw food prices directly impacts Malaysia’s economy. By detecting anomalies and predicting trends, our platform provides critical insights that can help stakeholders respond to market changes efficiently, ensuring a more stable food supply chain.
